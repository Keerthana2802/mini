Log In or Create Account

Get started by quickly logging in if you already have an account or creating a new one within minutes. Your account will give you access to our platform, where you can manage your donations, track their progress, and see the impact you're making in real time.

List Your Items

After logging in, you can start listing the items you wish to donate. Whether it's old clothes, non-perishable food, or other useful items, simply enter the details, and our system will take care of the rest. You can also view and manage all your listed items easily.

Pick Up By Volunteers

Once your items are listed, our trusted volunteers—affectionately known as "Pick-upBuddies"—will come to your doorstep to collect them. All pickups are scheduled based on your convenience, ensuring a hassle-free donation process.

Delivery and Distributions

Your donated items are transported to various trusted organizations and charities. These items are then distributed to those who need them the most, ensuring that your contributions make a direct impact.
